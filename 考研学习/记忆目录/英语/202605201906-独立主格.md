#### 独立主格的组成是什么?
- 名词 / 代词 + 非谓语动词 / 形容词 / 副词 / 介词短语
- 举例
	- 名词+doing: **The weather being cold**, we stayed inside.
	- 名词+done: **The work finished**, we went home.
	- 名词+形容词: **His face pale**, he walked into the room.
	- 名词+副词: **The meeting over**, everyone left.
	- 名词+介词短语: **Book in hand**, he entered the classroom.
<!--ID: 1779280276419-->


#### 独立主格本质是什么?
- 省略了连接词和 be 动词的状语从句.
	- Because the weather was fine, we went out. **The weather being fine**, we went out.
	- After the work was finished, we went home. **The work finished**, we went home.
<!--ID: 1779280276424-->


#### 独立主格跟分词状语有什么区别?
- 分词状语主语一致, 独立主格有自己的主语
	- **Walking down the street**, I saw an old friend.
